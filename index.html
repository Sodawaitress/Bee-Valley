When I was a child, I lived in a valley of my own mind.
Nobody else is actually there even though physically I have always lived in huge cities.
One day, the only people i invited into my valley left.
I couldn't bear staying in my valley alone again.
I left too, leaving my valley empty.
Then I moved into a shop, selling all kinds of my part
Just to figure out what my exact value are.
Five years later, I decided to walk out of that shop, go back to my valley.
I have the courage to live alone, and walk back to the shop again.
Welcome to my valley.
Weary, empty, hollow travellers of the universe.
May the peace be with you.
